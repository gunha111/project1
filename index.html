Japanese Ramen Ingredients

Key Ingredients

Noodles

Fresh, chewy noodles made from wheat flour, water, and kansui.

Broth

Rich base made from pork bones, chicken, seafood, or miso.

Chashu

Slow-cooked pork belly or shoulder, tender and flavorful.

Menma

Fermented bamboo shoots with a crunchy texture.

Egg

Soft-boiled egg marinated in soy sauce, often called ajitsuke tamago.

Green Onions

Freshly chopped green onions for a crisp, aromatic garnish.
